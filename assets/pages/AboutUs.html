About us

Canny are a global creative agency who help marketers do big things with their branding and websites!

Who are we?

We’re not just creatives, we’re collaborators. We work closely with our clients to connect their brands to their audience, by understanding their unique challenges and pain points. We’re down-to-earth and straight-talking and we pride ourselves on being an extension of your team.

As your partner, we’ll tell you how it is, even if it’s a little awkward. After all, you’re paying for our expertise so use it! We believe that collaboration is key, that’s why we treat your business and budget like our own.

Let’s work together to make the most impact and

achieve your goals.

We understand that marketers are constantly juggling multiple tasks and responsibilities.

That's why we partner with our clients to help them thrive in their role, instead of being overwhelmed and overworked. Our approach is simple: we take care of the hard work, so you can focus on the areas of your job that matter most.
